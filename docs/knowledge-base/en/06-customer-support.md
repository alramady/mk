# Customer Support â€” Common Issues & Troubleshooting

This guide provides Customer Support staff with instructions on how to handle common issues, troubleshoot problems, and determine when to escalate issues to the engineering team. A clear understanding of these processes is crucial for maintaining a high level of customer satisfaction and operational efficiency.

## 1. Core Responsibilities

The primary role of the **Support** team is to be the first point of contact for customers and internal staff regarding the "Monthly Key" platform. Key responsibilities include:

-   Responding to customer inquiries via all channels (phone, email, chat).
-   Troubleshooting and resolving common platform issues.
-   Guiding users on platform features and functionalities.
-   Escalating complex technical issues to the appropriate internal teams.
-   Documenting interactions and maintaining data integrity.

## 4. Security & Data Privacy

Protecting customer data is a top priority. All staff must adhere to the following security policies:

-   **NEVER** ask for or share customer payment information (full credit card numbers, CVV codes).
-   **NEVER** share internal API keys or credentials for Beds24 or Moyasar.
-   **ALWAYS** verify the identity of the person you are speaking with before discussing account details.
-   Report any suspicious activity or potential data breach to your manager and the **admin** team immediately.

See `../en/02-security-privacy.md` for the full security policy.

## 5. FAQ (Frequently Asked Questions)

**1. A customer says they paid but the system shows `DUE`. What do I do?**

First, politely ask for proof of payment. Then, check the Moyasar dashboard for a matching transaction. If you find no record in Moyasar, the payment was not successfully made through our platform, and you should guide the customer to try paying again.

**2. Why can't I approve a renewal for a customer?**

If the unit is managed by Beds24 (source is `BEDS24`), only an **admin** can approve the extension. This is because the change must be made manually in the Beds24 portal to prevent double bookings. Our system does not write data back to Beds24. You should forward the request to an admin.

**3. The system is slow or I see an error message. What should I do?**

First, try a hard refresh (Ctrl+Shift+R or Cmd+Shift+R). If the issue persists, take a screenshot of the error or the slow-loading page and try to replicate the issue. If it's a consistent problem, report it to engineering with the details and screenshot.
